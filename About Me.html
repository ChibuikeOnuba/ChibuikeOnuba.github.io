About Chibuike

My name is Onuba Chibuike Winner. I am a graduate from the University of Nigeria Nsukka (UNN). I studied Computer Science and majored in Data Science and Analysis.

I started my programming journey in 2020 durung the COVID-19 lockdown and since then, I have taken every challenge that comes with it.

My learning experience had its foundation on my coursework in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. So far, I have built on this by enrolling in Applied Data Science courses by Amazon, Microsoft and most recently by IBM. I also started working on the basic ML projects hich includes Iris flower Classification, Diabetes Prediction, COVID19 prediction, etc.

In 2021, I had the opportunity to work as a Data science intern at HamoyeAI where I went deeper into tuning ML models, learnt the basics of Computer Vision and worked on a regression project "Predicting Energy Efficiency of Buildings". Fast forward to 2023, I started working on open source projects, my first being building an ASD in toddlers prediction model in collaboration with OmdenaAI- a software organization based in Sri Lanka. This was a 3 months project and I was well satisfied because not only did improve my technical skills, it also improved my collaboration and communication skills as I had to work with Data Scientist from around the world.

During my summer break in 2023, I landed a Data Analysts role with TransAfam Power. I was found very helpful in the Procurement Sector due to my communication and bargainig skills. My daily activities involved the use of Ms Excel and its Power Query functions for Data entry, ETL and data manipulations, while using Python for automated tasks. My major accomplishment as a data analyst at TransAfam was my task on developing a Mid-Year Analysis Report for the department. This earned me an accolade before I had to return to campus to complete my BSc program.

I am currently exploring Natural Language Processing as I am currently working on a software - ReviewScope. ReviewScope is an innovative project leveraging Natural Language Processing (NLP) to analyze the sentiment embedded within customer reviews of a product by employing advanced NLP techniques. ReviewScope extracts valuable insights from customer feedback, shedding light on the overall sentiment towards the product and it features an interactive dashboard that provides users with a comprehensive overview of the sentiment expressed in the collected customer reviews.
